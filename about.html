My Story

Beauty has always been my love language.

I grew up in Charlotte — went to school here, fell in love here, built my career here. This city is in my bones, and every bride I work with gets that same care and rootedness. I'm not a vendor who flies in for the weekend. I'm your neighbor, your artist, your biggest fan on one of the most important mornings of your life.

"I want every bride to look in the mirror and feel like herself — just elevated, radiant, and completely ready."

I started doing makeup professionally over ten years ago, first in editorial and commercial work before finding my true calling in bridal. There's nothing quite like witnessing a bride's face when she sees herself for the first time on her wedding morning. That moment — that exhale — is why I do this.

My studio is in the heart of Charlotte, and I travel throughout the region for on-location services. I keep my calendar intentionally small so that every client gets my full attention, creativity, and care.

My Approach

Beauty that feels like you.

I believe bridal makeup should enhance, never mask. My goal is always to honor your natural features while giving you the confidence and glow that every wedding photo deserves. Whether your style is soft and romantic, bold and editorial, or somewhere beautifully in between — I'll bring it to life.

  • 🌿

    Natural First

    Your skin, your features, your personality — I build on what's already there, never over it.

  • 📷

    Photography-Tested

    Every product in my kit is chosen for how it performs in photos — because your memories deserve to look as beautiful as the moment.

  • 🤍

    Calm Presence

    Wedding mornings can be emotional. I bring a grounded, joyful energy to the room so you can breathe, laugh, and soak it all in.
