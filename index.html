The Artist

Beauty rooted in Charlotte.

I'm a Charlotte-based bridal makeup artist with over a decade of experience creating looks that photograph beautifully and feel effortlessly like you. My studio is in the heart of Charlotte, and I travel throughout the surrounding region for weddings.

My philosophy is simple: your wedding makeup should enhance who you already are — never mask it. Every bride I work with leaves feeling confident, radiant, and completely herself.
